About Me

I am a graphic designer and music producer from Tacoma, Washington. I came into college wanting to pursue computer science. After taking some classes I quickly realized that it wasn't for me. I picked up photoshop as a high-schooler so I was a little bit familiar with it. I got the opportunity in 2019 to start a job at EWU helping with recruiting graphics, as well as live-tweeting most of the sports events, and I really found my niche in this role.

As you can see in 'my work', I have made a few recruiting graphics for the EWU football team, as well as some various other graphics. I was also assigned the task of making a new header and profile picture for all of the EWU sports teams across various social media platforms including twitter, facebook, and instagram. Along with this, I have experience in live-tweeting, play by play, for verified accounts such as EWU Football with over 17k followers.

In mid 2020, I started producing music under the name cato. I have produced a few songs with people, as well as using my graphic design skills for cover art. One of my good friends has started releasing music under the name Isaac Alpough, and I've designed a few covers for him, and plan to continue working with him in the future.
